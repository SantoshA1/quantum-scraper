Quantum Engineering Trading — Architecture v5.9

Architect revision · July 7, 2026 · supersedes v5.8 (July 5) · ground-truthed against live workflow versions, Supabase state, and this week's shipped fixes
THE OUTCOME, FIRST: The architecture that beats Citadel is not the one with more layers, more AI models, or more nodes — it's the one already hiding inside v5.8: the evidence machine. Scanner → few, measured gates → cost-accounted execution → nightly reject-backtest → Conclave-gated promotion. Everything in v5.8 that feeds that loop is right and stays. Everything that bypasses it — vibes-scored discovery, unconsumed AI enrichment, unmeasured gates — is overplanned and gets mothballed or put on trial. v5.8's honest gap register already proved the point: the platform's losses have come from unmeasured machinery believed to be working, never from missing machinery. v5.9 therefore deletes ambition and adds proof. Citadel's edge is not 40 nodes; it is that nothing at Citadel runs unmeasured for three weeks.
